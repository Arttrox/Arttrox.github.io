...humans were colonizing space." (from Gravity - In the quest of colors). Gravity, is a series of games created by Arthur_code taking place in space, thousands of years after our era. The main character has a mysterious power, allowing him to reverse gravity at will.

  • Gravity

Gravity is the first opus of the serie. Released on May 13, 2021, the game lays the first base of the gameplay, similar to VVVVVV. With monsters, keys and spikes, the mechanics of gravity change remains however rather unexploited in this first project. Gravity is still a very good game, which can be finished in a few minutes.


  • Gravity - In the quest of colors

The second game of the "Gravity" series has not even been announced yet. It will probably be released on 2022. The title lets us think that the protagonist will go in search of colors, in a black and white world. In short, this game will be full of surprises.
